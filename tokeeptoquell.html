  • To Keep You Up At Night / To Quell Your Worried Mind is an attempt to help us as humans to recontextualize our existence within the context of the universe. As a species who often gets caught up in the mundane, it is important that we take a step back and realize that we are a small part of something greater than we can ever imagine.

    Taking its form as a zine, To Keep / To Quell is a merging of art and science. The first issue deals with the recycling of particles within the universe, while the second addresses our perceptions vs. the reality of time.
