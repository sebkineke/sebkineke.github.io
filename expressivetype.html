  • The Great Sex Letter is womanizer Neal Cassady's drunken letter to Jack Kerouac. In it he describes his infatuation and lust for two girls he meets on a bus. In this piece, Neal's stream of consciousness is portrayed through expressive typography, and his words are paired with lyrics to The Ying Yang Twins' 2005 hit "Wait (The Whisper Song)". While the lyrics are meant to voice the author's subconcious, the pairing of the texts also allows a connection to be drawn between the rampant objectification of women 50 years ago and today.

Seb made it. 2015.
