Spiced Hot Apple Cider

  • 5 min prep
  • 8 min cool time
  • serves 15

INGREDIENTS*

  • 1/4 c. pached brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. whole allspice
  • 1 tsp. whole cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 large orange, wedged
  • 1/4 tsp. morton's salt
  • 2 quart apple cider

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. 1Turn saucepan on medium heat and pour cider in
  2. 2Mix together brown sugar, allspice, cloves, cinnamon stick, salt, nutmeg, and orange wedges in a filter over serving jar
  3. 3As cider begins to simmer, slowly pour through filter into jar
  4. 4Repeat 3 times, serve hot

*To add a little pep in your step, add 1 1/2 cups light rum to simmering pan
