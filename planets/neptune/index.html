Neptune

The Ice Giant Pt. II

Neptune is the eighth and technically the last planet from the Sun in the Solar System. What's super crazy about this planet is that it was the first planet found by mathematical prediction rather than by someone being like "wow I see a planet here check it out". It was discovered when a guy named Alexis Bouvard noticed that Uranus' orbit was being effected by an object so large that it had to be a planet. Neptune was seen for reals in September 1846. This planet, though farther from the sun than Uranus, is a tad warmer. So pull out those board shorts, the temperature here gets to a balmy average of -328°F.

Age: 4.5 billion years

Surface tempurature: -328°F

Size compared to Earth: 63 Earths could fit inside of this planet
