Saturn

The Ringed Planet

Saturn is definitely the coolest looking planet of them all. Like, it has rings for Chrissakes. It is named after the Roman God of Arcitecture, which is slightly less cool, but Saturn is making it work. Its outer atmosphere is generally steady and unchanging, because Saturn has its crap together. It is another one of the Gas Giants. Saturn's ring system consists of nine rings (because it is the number one bae), all of which are made up of ice particles with a smaller amount of rocky debris and dust. And they have have moons. That's right, in addition to Saturns hefty collection of 62 moons, there are a numerous supply of even more moons clinging onto its rings. One of Saturn's largest moons is Titan, which is larger than Mercury and has it's own atmosphere. It's living the American Dream.

Age: 4.5 billion years

Surface tempurature: -288°F

Size compared to Earth: 763 Earths could fit inside of Saturn
