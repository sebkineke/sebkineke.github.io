Earth

The Home Planet

Earth is the largest out of four terrestrial planets, and, to our knowledge, the only one that supports life. There are so many living things on Earth. There are snakes, sea monkeys, Redwood trees, grandmothers, lions, tulips, worms, Tony Hawk, yams, playground bullies, cacti, ferrets, toy dogs, brain-eating amoebas, REO Speedwagon, bedbugs, donkeys, butterflies, and possibly the Lochness Monster.

But the thing is, living things are a new addition to Earth. It existed for a very long time as a unlivable bubbling mass of molten rock, and it took millions and millions of years to become the planet we know and love today. Humans must remember that they are a very small part of something much larger - something they always seem to forget.
