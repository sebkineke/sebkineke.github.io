  • With a father who spent a great deal of his life on a sailboat and a grandfather who has built countless ships, the sea is in my blood. A Sailor's Life is an homage to my family and to the history of the sea. It deals specifically with the life of sailors during the 19th century, one of the most romanticised periods of maritime history.

    The playful illustrations and the large text are meant to make it seem like a child's book. The content is rather gruesome, however, as it deals with things like drinking problems and limbs falling off.

Seb made it. 2015.
