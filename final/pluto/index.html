Pluto

A Planet in Our Hearts

Discovered in 1930, Pluto was originally classified as the ninth planet from the Sun. Its status as a major planet started being questioned 75 years later, when scientists started thinking "this planet is too darn small." Starting in 1977 with the discovery of the minor planet Chiron, numerous icy objects similar to Pluto with eccentric orbits were found. Pluto's distance from Earth makes in-depth investigation difficult. Many details about Pluto will remain unknown until 2015, when the New Horizons spacecraft is expected to arrive there.
